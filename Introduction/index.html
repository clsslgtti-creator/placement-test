English Language Placement Test

Please read the information below before you begin the assessment.

What to Expect

The placement test is divided into five timed sections. You must complete each section in order. Once a section starts, the timer counts down until the allotted time is finished.

  • 1

    Grammar

    40 minutes • 50 multiple-choice questions on grammar accuracy.

  • 2

    Reading

    15 minutes • Read a passage and answer comprehension questions.

  • 3

    Listening

    20 minutes • Two listening tasks. Headphones are recommended.

  • 4

    Writing

    20 minutes • Two writing tasks tailored to your training programme.

  • 5

    Vocabulary

    5 minutes • Match key terms with the correct definitions.

How the Timers Work

Automatic start. Each timer begins as soon as a section loads. Review the instructions first, then begin answering without delay.
Live countdown. Time remaining is displayed at the top of every page together with a progress bar.
Automatic submission. When the timer reaches zero the section closes and your answers are submitted automatically.
No pauses or restarts. Do not refresh or close the window. Leaving the page may end the section and your progress cannot be recovered.

Before You Begin

  • Check your audio.

    Test your headphones or speakers. You will only hear the listening tracks twice.

  • Stable connection.

    Use a reliable internet connection and avoid switching devices during the test.

  • Work independently.

    This placement determines the correct starting level. Please answer without assistance.
